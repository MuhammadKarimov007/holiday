East or West, Sakrisoy is always best!

We know that you want to escape the hustle-bustles of big cities and enjoy the fascinating nature. We will help you achieve this because we are certain that Sakrisoy is the right place for you. Then, what are you waiting for! Pack up your things and visit our Hometown.

Top three activities you can enjoy during your stay

Ice Caves

Once inside the cave, you will explore the wonders around you! The natural light peaking through the dense ice is a dreamy wintry sight. You will be free to explore the cave at your own pace while wearing a helmet for added safety. The beautiful and endless shades of blue will provide the perfect backdrop for pictures!

Sleep in real Igloos

Imagine sleeping way out in the Greenlandic nature, where the silence of nature creates a beautiful backdrop for your stay. Maybe you will hear the wind and the crackle of snow while lying on a blanket and looking at the yellowish glow of a candle.

Ice Fishing

Try ice fishing for the first time! On a safe frozen lake or river outside Sakrisoy, you will learn the basics of ice fishing, drill your hole and try to catch a fish like a local! Good luck!
